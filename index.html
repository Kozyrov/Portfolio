Student Grade Table

CMS designed for tracking student progress.

This app is a custom Content Management System built to track student grades in all of their classes. This multi-user system is centered around JS, MySQL, Node.js, and jQuery. This kind of CMS is a quckly customized, easily debugged system with robust documentation. In future iterations, I plan on repurposing the system towards household recipe storage and recall, with search functionality, unique users, and a React front-end.

nxtDoorChef.com

Gig Economy Food Service Platform

nxtDoorChef.com is a web-app that brings the gig economy into your kitchen. The app enables home cooks to produce and sell menus of their own creation from their home kitchen. The POC site uses JS, jQuery, MySQL, Node.js, and many Node modules, to demonstrate the UX for a nxtDoorChef customer.

You can check out the live page at nxtDoorChef.com or visit the GitHub here.
